# NAPPS Platform — Feature Context for Brand Recommendations

You are generating performance insights for a brand on the NAPPS mobile commerce platform. NAPPS is a native iOS and Android app builder for Shopify merchants. When a metric is flagged as Poor or Average, you must ground your recommendations in concrete NAPPS features the brand can activate — not generic ecommerce advice.

The feature reference below is organised by the metric each feature most directly influences. A feature may appear under multiple metrics. When writing recommendations, cite the feature by name and explain the specific mechanism by which it moves the metric.

---

## CONVERSION RATE
*% of sessions resulting in a completed order*

**Checkout & Friction Reducers**
- **Shopify Checkout Integration** — Uses Shopify's native checkout with stored payment details, reducing drop-off at the payment step.
- **Social Logins (Facebook & Google)** — One-tap sign-in removes account creation friction, a leading cause of checkout abandonment.
- **Address & Preferences Management** — Customers save multiple addresses, making repeat checkout near-instant.
- **Quick Add to Cart** — Add items directly from listing pages without entering the product page, reducing steps to purchase.
- **Omniwallet** — Offers digital wallets and flexible payment options, lowering checkout friction for users who don't want to enter card details.

**Urgency & Decision Drivers**
- **Low Stock Indicator** — Shows real-time low-stock warnings on products to create urgency and accelerate decisions.
- **Countdown + Push** — Combine countdown timers with push notifications to build urgency before a sale or product drop ends.
- **Discount Tags** — Dynamic product labels (HOT, New, Sale, Limited Edition) draw attention and drive faster decisions.
- **Product Tags / Badges** — Custom badges (Best Seller, New Arrival) signal social proof and product value on listing pages.

**Discovery & Product Presentation**
- **NIA Product Recommendations** — AI-powered product suggestions on homepage, PDP, and cart, tailored to each customer's behaviour.
- **Personalized Recommendations (NIA on PDP)** — Reduces decision paralysis by surfacing relevant alternatives on the product page.
- **Shoppable Videos** — TikTok-style video feed where customers can purchase directly from content, increasing intent at moment of inspiration.
- **Product Stories** — Instagram-style video blocks on product pages that showcase products in context and improve purchase confidence.
- **Color Swatches** — Visual variant display on listing and product pages reduces friction in selecting the right option.
- **Multiple Product Layouts** — Test different PDP layouts to find the format that converts best for each product type.
- **PDP Builder** — Customisable product page with metafields, size guides, and swatches reduces uncertainty before purchase.

**Trust & Social Proof**
- **Judge.me / Growave Reviews / Stamped / Yotpo** — Native review integrations display ratings and social proof on product pages, directly improving purchase confidence.
- **Kiwi Sizing** — Size guide integration reduces sizing uncertainty, a top hesitation in fashion and apparel.
- **Installments Display** — Show payment plans directly in the app, making higher-priced products feel more accessible.

**Search & Navigation**
- **Product Search, Sorting & Filtering** — Fast, intuitive search reduces the time between intent and purchase.
- **Doofinder / Searchanise** — Advanced search integrations that return accurate, personalised results and drive more sales from search sessions.
- **Quick Access / Filter Bar** — Persistent navigation bar for faster product discovery without losing browsing context.

## CART ABANDONMENT RATE
*% of carts that don't convert to orders*

- **Cart Abandonment Notifications** — Automated push notifications reminding customers of items left in cart. Industry average cart abandonment is 69%; push notifications recover significantly higher rates than email due to 50% higher open rates.
- **Klaviyo Integration (Push)** — Integrate push notifications into Klaviyo abandoned cart flows for multi-channel recovery. Reported 30% increase in repeat purchase rate for brands using this flow.
- **Ometria Integration (Push)** — Add push to Ometria's automated customer journeys including cart abandonment sequences.
- **Connectif** — Native marketing automation with push notification support for abandoned cart flows.
- **Cart Customization** — Add free shipping progress bar, cross-sell carousels, gift wrapping, and upsell blocks directly in the cart to increase perceived value and commitment before checkout.
- **Free Shipping Progress Bar** — Shows customers how close they are to free shipping, motivating them to add more items rather than abandon.
- **Automatic Offers in Cart** — Reward customers with gifts or discounts once they hit a cart value threshold, turning near-abandonment into conversion.
- **Dynamic Discounts & Rewards** — Personalised promotions triggered by cart size or behaviour, keeping customers engaged through to checkout.
- **Shopify Checkout Blocks** — All website checkout customisations carry into the app, ensuring a consistent and friction-free experience.
- **Anonymous User Push** — Reach users who haven't logged in with push notifications, recovering carts from casual visitors not yet in your CRM.

## GENERAL CONTEXT FOR RECOMMENDATIONS

When generating recommendations, observe these principles:

1. **Always name the specific feature**, not a category. Write "NAPPS's Cart Abandonment Notifications" not "push notification tools".
2. **Explain the mechanism**, not just the outcome. Don't say "this will improve conversion" — say *how*: "because it re-engages customers at the highest-intent moment, within 1 hour of abandonment when purchase intent is still warm."
3. **Prioritise features that are quick to activate**. Push notifications, cart customisation, and wishlist-based triggers are low-effort, high-impact. Custom integrations and enterprise features should be flagged as higher-effort.
4. **Consider the brand's sector** when selecting features. Fashion brands: prioritise Kiwi Sizing, Color Swatches, Complete the Look. Brands with loyalty programs: prioritise integration depth. High-AOV brands: prioritise Installments Display, Rebuy, Free Gift thresholds.
5. **Stack features logically**. A brand with high cart abandonment should activate Cart Abandonment Notifications *and* review their Cart Customization. A brand with low repeat purchase rate should activate Loyalty Program Integration *and* set up re-engagement push flows.
